Space run




Hi dear friend, my name is Artem! I welcome you in my game "Space run". The rules are simple, you need to get to their base but in your way will meet asteroids, your task will be to avoid the blow. But do not think that it's simple. Through steady period of time will increase the speed of the asteroid, and you will have only three lives. Controls are simple A - move left, D - move right. That's all. Good luck!
